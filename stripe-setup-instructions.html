Stripe Payment Setup

Follow these steps to set up payments for the Auburn Business Network

Part 1: Create Your Stripe Account

1
Go to stripe.com and click "Start now" to create a free account.
2
Enter your email address and create a password.
3
Complete the business verification — Stripe will ask for your business name, EIN or SSN, and business address.
4
Add your bank account (routing number + account number) so Stripe can deposit payments to you.
Why do you need your own Stripe account? This ensures all listing fee payments ($300/yr) go directly into your bank account. Williams Digital's Stripe account is separate and only handles the platform fee.

Part 2: Create 4 Payment Links

You need 4 payment links for different pricing tiers that the admin can select when approving businesses.

1
In Stripe, go to Product catalog (left sidebar) → click + Add product
2
Create a product called "Auburn Business Network — Annual Listing"
3
Add 4 prices to this product (click "+ Add another price" for each):
Price NameAmountBilling
Full Price$300.00Recurring / Year
25% Off$225.00Recurring / Year
50% Off$150.00Recurring / Year
75% Off$75.00Recurring / Year
4
For each price, go to Payment Links (left sidebar) → + Create payment link → select the price → Create link
5
Make sure "Collect email address" is turned ON for each payment link (this is how we match payments to businesses)
Send us all 4 payment links. They will look like: https://buy.stripe.com/xxxxxxx. We'll add them to the admin dashboard so the correct link is sent based on the pricing tier selected.

Part 3: Set Up the Webhook

The webhook tells our system when a business has paid, so their listing goes live automatically.

1
In Stripe, go to Developers (bottom-left or top-right) → Webhooks
2
Click "+ Add endpoint"
3
Enter this URL as the endpoint:
FieldValue
Endpoint URLhttps://buyauburn.com/api/stripe-webhook
Events to listen tocheckout.session.completed
4
For "Events to send", click "+ Select events" → search for checkout.session.completed → check it → click "Add events"
5
Click "Add endpoint" to save
6
After creating, click on the webhook endpoint → find "Signing secret" → click "Reveal" → copy it
Send us the webhook signing secret. It will look like: whsec_xxxxxxxxxxxxxxx. We need this to verify that payment notifications are really from Stripe.
